This is Bears Baseball

In 2011 the team that would eventually become the Utah Bears was formed. The team was known as the Twins until 2015. In 2015 the team manager retired from the game and the reins were handed over to Tim Wade. Under Tim, the team name was changed to the Salt Lake Avalanche.

As the Salt Lake Avalanche the team has taken 2nd in the league 2 times.

In the fall of 2019 the team once again changed its identity to the Utah Bears after the team of the same name from the Walter Mathau classic "Bad News Bears". This is a reflection of who we are as a team. We aren't polished, or posh, but we have guts, fight, and can throw em back when we need to!
